# 6. Development Process & Team Dynamics

This section describes *how the team worked*, as opposed to what they built. It
covers the development methodology, the tools used to coordinate, the version
control strategy the team adopted, and the structure of the team itself with each
member's contributions.

The project was developed over **eleven weeks**, and its history is documented
week by week in the [Weeks](../week_01.md) section of this book. This chapter
distills the working practices behind that timeline.
