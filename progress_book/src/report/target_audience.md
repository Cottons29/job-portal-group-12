# 2.3. Target Audience

The platform serves two primary user groups, plus a supporting administrative
role.

### Students (job seekers)
The core audience: university and high-school students looking for flexible,
part-time, or short-term work that fits around their studies. They value quick
discovery, relevant recommendations, transparency about application status, and
the ability to present verified skills and experience.

### Employers (job posters)
Local businesses, startups, and organizations that need part-time staff. They
need to publish clear listings, reach qualified students, review applicants in a
structured pipeline, and communicate with candidates directly.

### Administrators
A supporting role responsible for trust and safety — most notably verifying
student identities through uploaded student ID cards, and moderating content
where necessary.

The dual-audience nature of the platform shaped the design of the unified profile
module, the role-aware navigation, and the recruitment pipeline, all of which
must serve students and employers from opposite sides of the same workflow.
