# 2. Introduction

Finding trustworthy part-time work is a recurring challenge for students:
opportunities are scattered across social media, messaging groups, and notice
boards, with little structure, no verification, and no reliable way to track an
application after it is sent. The **Student Part-Time Job Portal** was built to
consolidate this experience into a single, purpose-built platform.

This section frames the project: the **problem** it addresses, the **objectives**
the team set out to achieve, the **audiences** it serves, and the **metrics** used
to judge whether it succeeded.

The platform is intentionally more than a directory of listings. It treats job
seeking as a social and iterative activity — students build a profile, follow
employers and peers, discover relevant roles through search and recommendations,
apply, and then communicate in real time as their application moves through a
recruitment pipeline. The following subsections expand on each of these framing
points.
